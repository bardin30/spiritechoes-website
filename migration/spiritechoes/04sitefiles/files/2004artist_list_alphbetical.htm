Congratulations to our “Showcase Artist”

Dale Lewis

Booth # 216

 

The Spirit Echoes Fine Art Festival is proud to present Dale

Lewis of Oneonta, Alabama as the 2004 Showcase Artist.

 

Dale works alone in his studio creating unique pieces inspired

by visual stimuli or images drawn from his personal experiences.

His work is based around exceptional pieces of wood. Often

whimsical, the playful nature if each piece is often emphasized by

bright colors and exaggerated features which are refined to a

state of elegance.

 

Each piece is constructed by methods common to typical furniture

construction:  mortise and tenon, dovetails, and lap joints.  He also

uses screws, bolts, and brackets to make the pieces stronger and more

functional.  The shaping and carving that characterizes his work is

done with power saws, grinders, sanders and carvers.  Refining is

accomplished with manual tools and meticulous handwork.

 

Dale has exhibited across the United States and has been featured in

many prestigious shows including but not limited to:  the Montgomery,

Huntsville, Columbus, and Mobile Museums of Fine Art,  the James A.

Renwick Alliance Craft Auction in Washington, D.C., the Tennessee

Valley Art Association Solo Exhibit, and the Kentuck Festival.  His

awards and publications are extensive and too numerous to list.  We

truly appreciate his participation in the Spirit Echoes first annual fine

art festival and are delighted to share his work with you.
